Tanzania Technology Community (TTC) is a students and Non student Organization that is basically formed to improve and enhance technical skill level of young Tanzanians who wants to do more activity with their talents and professionals in the field of COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY, DATA SCIENCE AND STATISTICS in personal development programs through training, Workshops,Seminars and live activities from industrial environment by extracting the hidden talents in collaboration with leading Software Industries.

Also, training young Tanzanians researchers to analyze massive data and increase need for industrial professionalism to interpret data and help companies for better business decision making.

This include; Seniors, Experts, Advanced, intermediate and Beginners Both in Office, Street and School within the Country (Even if you didn’t learn it at school but you have interest in it, Do not Worry, this is for you to grow up, Remember "what you find in you speaks louder and powerful than what you find in School")
